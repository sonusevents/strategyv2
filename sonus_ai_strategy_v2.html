Executive Summary

10 KEY TRUTHS
01 // VISION
Lean and lethal
5–7 humans + always-on AI layer handling 80% of grunt work: outreach, content, quoting, SEO, scheduling, and lead qualification. Achievable now.
02 // PRIORITY
Sales is the biggest bleeding wound
Lauren, Gill, a manual pipeline. No scalable outbound, no content engine, no automated follow-up. A sales infrastructure problem — not a product problem.
03 // CORE STACK
N8N is your nervous system
Every system connects through N8N on Contabo VPS. Priority: Clay → verification → GHL → outreach → reply handling → booking.
04 // INTELLIGENCE
Clay + Claude Code = your BDE
Clay enriches; Claude Code personalises — angle generation, proposal drafts, reply analysis. Together they replace a £40k/yr BDE for outbound.
05 // CONTENT
Content is a compounding asset
Apify scraping + AEO-optimised SEO targeting AI search engines. Inbound leads within 60–90 days is realistic with consistent output.
06 // MOAT
3D visualisation is your competitive moat
LiDAR → Polycam → OpenArt → UE5 renders in proposals. Almost no UK AV company doing this. Shortens sales cycles and wins high-value bids.
07 // INFRA
Stack is already 70% there
GHL, N8N, Clay, HeyReach, Current RMS all exist. Gap = automation logic, trained prompts, and consistent workflows connecting them.
08 // SEO
AEO is the 2025–2026 SEO play
Structure content so Perplexity, ChatGPT Search, and Google AI Overviews cite you. 14 landing pages are the foundation. Add FAQ schema now.
09 // TALENT
Prostage arms are strategic assets
Academy and Crew with AI-assisted CV screening, interview pre-qual, and SOP onboarding — 80% of recruitment pipeline before a human touches it.
10 // FIRST 30 DAYS
Plumbing not polish
Not the website. Not the rebrand. Start: outbound in Clay + HeyReach, N8N lead-to-CRM workflow, proposal template in Claude + Current RMS.

Bottlenecks to Attack First

6 CRITICAL
Sales — No Scalable Outbound
Reactive pipeline only. No proactive machine generating new conversations.
→ System 01 · Week 1 priority
Quoting — Too Slow, Too Manual
Every proposal = 3–4hrs of Lauren's time. Bottleneck to sales velocity.
→ System 04 · Weeks 2–3
Marketing — No Content Output
LinkedIn, TikTok, blog underutilised. No rhythm. Competitors building audiences.
→ System 03 · Week 5
Customer Service — Reactive Comms
No automated check-ins, pre-event briefings, or post-event feedback.
→ N8N GHL post-sale workflow · Week 4
Design — No Visual Proposals
Proposals lack visual impact. Competitors with renders winning on presentation alone.
→ System 05 + 04 · Week 6
Email — No Deliverability Infra
No dedicated sending infrastructure, no warm-up. Risk of spam before it's read.
→ Cloudflare domains + Instantly · Day 1
